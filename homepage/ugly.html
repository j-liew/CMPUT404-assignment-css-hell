About Me

I’m Connor McDavid and I’m a hockey player from Newmarket, Ontario.

I currently serve as captain for the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League.

My History

I started playing hockey when I was 4 years old.

I played junior hockey for the Erie Otters of the Ontario Hockey League from 2012-2015.

In 2015 I was picked first overall by the Edmonton Oilers in the NHL entry draft and have played for them ever since.

In 2016 I was named captain of the Oilers, making me the youngest person ever to captain an NHL team.
